Welcome to my Sushi Recipe!

Description

Sushi is the best! There are many different types of sushi to enjoy, but here we have the most popular sushi rolls. These are Salmon rolls with avacado.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Seaweed
  • Eel Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay out the Seaweed
  2. Lay out the Rice ontop of the seaweed
  3. Add the Salmon
  4. Add the Avocado
  5. Roll it together into a roll
  6. Pour Eel Sauce
