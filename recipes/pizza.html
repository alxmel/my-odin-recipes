Welcome to my Pizza Recipe!

Description

Here is the delicious pizza recipe! We need tomatoes, cheese, dough, and finally pepperoni! We will also be needing a brick oven to cook the pizza in. Then you will be able to enjoy this goodness!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Spread the Dough
  2. Spread the Tomato Sauce
  3. Pour the Cheese
  4. Add the Pepperoni
  5. Bake!
