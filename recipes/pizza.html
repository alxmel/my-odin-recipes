Welcome to my Pizza Recipe!

Description

Here is the delicious pizza recipe in all of it's cheesy and pepperoni goodness! The ingredeints include: Tomatoes, Cheese, Dough, Pepperoni. Another very important item needed in this recipe is a Brick oven to cook the pizza in! This adds a layer of flavor that will make you further enjoy this gooey goodness!

  • Preperation Time

    45 minutes

  • Difficulty

    Medium

  • Tastiness

    5/5

Steps

  1. Spread the Dough
  2. Spread the Tomato Sauce
  3. Pour the Cheese
  4. Add the Pepperoni
  5. Bake!
